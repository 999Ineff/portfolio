Want PDF Versions?

  1. Open any guide in your browser
  2. Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac)
  3. Destination: "Save as PDF"
  4. Check "Background graphics"
  5. Set Margins to "None"
  6. Uncheck "Headers and footers"
  7. Save

Each guide has print instructions at the top that disappear in the PDF.
